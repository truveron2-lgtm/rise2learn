Bridging the Digital Gap for Vulnerable Children in Africa.

Digital Empowerment & Skills Development for Underserved Schools.

Welcome To Rise2Learn Official Website

The project is designed to introduce practical access to technology, foundational digital skills, and long-term capacity building for students and educators who currently have limited or no exposure to digital learning tools.

Our goal is to ensure that children are not left behind in an increasingly digital world and that schools are equipped to prepare students for future learning and opportunities.

Rise2Learn addresses the structural challenges faced by many public and community schools, including lack of digital infrastructure, limited ICT knowledge, and insufficient training support for educators.

Through a combination of digital skills training and guided technology adoption, the initiative helps schools transition into functional digital learning environments that support modern teaching and learning practices.

By focusing on sustainability, local capacity, and practical usage, Rise2Learn aims to create lasting impact that extends beyond initial support and empowers schools to continue growing independently.

Rise2Learn Training Components

The training component of Rise2Learn focuses on building practical digital skills for both students and teachers. Students are introduced to basic computer usage, digital literacy, and responsible technology use. Training emphasizes hands-on learning and step-by-step understanding to build confidence and curiosity. Teachers receive capacity-building support to help them integrate technology into classroom teaching, manage digital tools, and support students in ongoing learning beyond the duration of the project.

Student Digital Skills Training

Students are introduced to basic computer usage, digital literacy, and responsible technology use, helping them build confidence and curiosity through guided, hands-on learning.

Hands-On & Step-by-Step Learning

Digital Literacy & Responsibility

Learners gain essential knowledge on safe, responsible, and productive use of digital tools, preparing them to navigate technology confidently in school and beyond.

Teacher Capacity Building

Teachers receive targeted training to strengthen their digital skills and confidence, enabling them to effectively integrate technology into everyday classroom teaching.

How to Apply

The application process follows five simple steps. To begin, you must first express interest by sending us an email. Other stages will follow after review.

Step 1: Send an Expression of Interest

Send an email to info@rise2learn.org to indicate your interest in participating in the Rise2Learn program.

Step 2: Receive Application Link

After reviewing your email, we will send you a link to the official application form directly to your email address.

Step 3: Complete the Application Form

Fill out the form with accurate details about your school, organization, or personal background to complete your application.

Step 4: Application Review

Our team carefully reviews all submissions to ensure alignment with the program goals and priority needs.

Step 5: Confirmation & Next Steps

Successful applicants will receive a confirmation email with further instructions, timelines, and onboarding details.
